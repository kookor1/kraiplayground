KR AI Playground Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how KR AI Playground may collect, use, and protect limited information in connection with its website, public pages, and related services.

1. Information collected

KR AI Playground may collect limited non-personal information such as browser type, device type, referral source, visited pages, basic analytics data, and other standard technical information provided by browsers, hosting platforms, or analytics services.

2. Information you provide

If you contact the project operator directly, submit a form, open an issue, or otherwise provide information voluntarily, that information may be used to respond to your inquiry or support the service.

3. How information may be used

Information may be used to:

  • operate and improve the project and its public pages;
  • understand usage patterns and troubleshoot issues;
  • respond to questions, reports, or support requests;
  • protect the service against abuse, fraud, or misuse.

4. Cookies and third-party services

KR AI Playground may rely on hosting providers, analytics tools, embedded services, or platform integrations that use cookies or similar technologies. Those third parties may process limited technical information under their own privacy policies.

5. Data sharing

KR AI Playground does not intentionally sell personal information. Limited information may be processed by third-party service providers where required to host, secure, analyze, or operate the service.

6. External links

The service may link to third-party websites, platforms, or tools. KR AI Playground is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external services.

7. Data retention

Information may be retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, respond to requests, meet legal obligations, or resolve technical and security issues.

8. Children’s privacy

KR AI Playground is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law, and it does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Updates to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version published on this page will be treated as the current version.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use the project repository or issue tracker: github.com/kookor1/kraiplayground.
